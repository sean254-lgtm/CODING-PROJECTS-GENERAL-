Chapati recipe

This is a simple step by step procedure on how to make it,, pay attention

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Sugar
  • Water
  • Cooking oil

Instructions

  1. Mix the flour with the following ingredients
  2. Wait for the mixture to dry for sometime
  3. Separate the mixtute into several smaller pieces and roll them to a flat circle
  4. Finally fry the dough, serve it and enjoy the meal
